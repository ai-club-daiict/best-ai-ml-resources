### Build a Large Language Model (From Scratch) — Sebastian Raschka

* One of the best books for understanding the internal architecture of Large Language Models (LLMs), including the Transformer architecture, attention mechanisms, tokenization, embeddings, and the complete training pipeline.

* The book builds an LLM entirely from scratch using Python, helping you understand every component instead of treating it as a black box.

* After completing this book, you will be able to:

  * Understand how modern LLMs such as **DeepSeek**, **OpenGLM**, **Llama**, **Qwen**, and **GPT-style** models work internally.
  * Build and train your own Transformer-based language model from scratch.
  * Read and understand research papers on LLMs more effectively.
  * Customize, fine-tune, and experiment with open-source LLM architectures.

### Hands-On Large Language Models: Language Understanding and Generation — Jay Alammar & Maarten Grootendorst

* One of the best practical books for understanding how Large Language Models (LLMs) work and how to apply them to real-world NLP and AI applications.

* The book provides a strong foundation in LLM concepts, covering the internal architecture of language models, Transformer-based models, embeddings, and modern NLP techniques with clear explanations and hands-on examples.

* It covers a wide range of topics, including:

  * Transformer architecture and attention mechanisms
  * How modern LLMs work internally
  * Text classification
  * Text clustering and topic modeling
  * Embeddings and semantic search
  * Prompt engineering
  * Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
  * LangChain and LLM application development
  * Fine-tuning and adapting language models for downstream tasks

* After completing this book, you will be able to:

  * Understand the architecture and working principles of modern LLMs.
  * Build practical NLP applications using state-of-the-art language models.
  * Apply embeddings for semantic search, retrieval, clustering, and recommendation systems.
  * Use prompt engineering and LangChain to develop production-ready AI applications.
  * Gain the knowledge needed to work with open-source LLMs and build intelligent AI systems.
